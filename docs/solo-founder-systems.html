The New Kind of Founder

Once, it took a village to build a startup. Designers shaped the brand, engineers built the code, marketers told the story. In the age of generative intelligence, those boundaries blur. One founder can hold the entire orchestra — thinking, building, designing, deploying, storytelling — all at once. That founder is not superhuman. They are system‑human.

The Philosophy

A single human, with the right architecture of tools, agents, and rituals, can create value at the scale once reserved for companies of many.
